What are the Quests?

A treasure hunt woven into the Experimentation Elite conference. Twenty-five participants split into five teams of five. Take on five sensory challenges spread across the venue, each one rooted in an experimentation concept. Collect coins and work together to come out on top.

One team wins. Each member of the winning team takes home a £125 prize. Every participant gets 10 trees planted in their name. And you won't miss a single session or conversation to take part.

Orientation at 8:00 AM in Darden Room · Quests kick off at 11:00 AM
