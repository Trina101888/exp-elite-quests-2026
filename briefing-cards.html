Team 1 — Red

Five challenges. Five senses.
Please work together and be present with your team.

  1. Quests go live at 11:00 AM. You have until 2:00 PM.
  2. Follow your sequence below. Each team has a different route.
  3. On each floor, find the Quest Master — look for the badge.
  4. Complete the quest and submit your answers to the Quest Master. Get it right, earn a coin.
  5. Each team member should own one quest. Your matching wristbands make it easy to find each other across floors.
  6. Once all quests are done, return to Darden (3rd Floor) immediately. Fastest team with the most coins wins.
